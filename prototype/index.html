Exploring the Natural State’s Wild Side

Arkansas truly lives up to its nickname, The Natural State, offering some of the most diverse and rugged outdoor experiences in the Midwest and South. It is imperative that all visitors practice Leave No Trace principles.

The Buffalo National River Region

Established in 1972 as America’s first national river, this destination is a paddler's paradise. Many veteran explorers consider the Buffalo a must-do trip.

"You haven’t truly seen Arkansas until you’ve floated under the towering cliffs of the Buffalo."

Key Hiking Trails in the Upper Region

The area around Jasper and Ponca offers access to incredible trails. As the locals say, The climb is worth the view.
