Frequently Asked Questions

How do rovers generate power?

Both modern rovers use MMRTG (nuclear) power systems to ensure consistent operation during Martian nights and dust storms.

Is there water on Mars?

Rovers have confirmed that Mars had liquid water on its surface in the distant past and currently holds ice at its poles.
